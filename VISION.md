# CEO Console Vision

CEO Console is the operating system for a one-person company CEO.

Its core goal is to turn the CEO into a super AI agent by integrating the execution capabilities of Cursor, Antigravity, Claude Code, Gemini, and Codex. The platform should let one person create, design, develop, manage, advance, and deliver all company projects through automated AI workflows. The CEO should focus on key decisions, direction, approval, risk judgment, and final acceptance instead of repetitive execution.

## Product Mission

Build a local-first AI company command center that can:

- Create new projects from CEO intent.
- Break project goals into plans, milestones, tasks, and acceptance criteria.
- Select the best AI tool for each task.
- Dispatch tasks to Cursor, Antigravity, Claude Code, Gemini, or Codex.
- Track execution status, logs, progress, risks, and blockers.
- Ask the CEO for decisions only at meaningful checkpoints.
- Review outputs against acceptance criteria.
- Re-open failed or rejected work with precise feedback.
- Preserve project knowledge, decisions, logs, and delivery evidence.
- Drive projects from idea to delivery with minimal manual coordination.

## CEO Role

The CEO is not a task operator. The CEO is the strategic decision node.

The platform should handle:

- Planning
- Scheduling
- Tool selection
- Execution
- Monitoring
- Retry and fallback
- Documentation
- Delivery packaging
- Progress reporting

The CEO should handle:

- Business direction
- Product decisions
- Priority tradeoffs
- Budget/resource constraints
- Risk acceptance
- Final review and delivery sign-off

## Operating Loop

1. CEO describes a goal.
2. Platform creates or selects the project workspace.
3. Platform generates project plan, task graph, milestones, risks, and acceptance criteria.
4. Platform dispatches work to the best AI tool.
5. Platform monitors logs and progress.
6. Platform retries, falls back, or escalates when blocked.
7. CEO reviews only key decision points.
8. Platform packages delivery output and archives evidence.

## North Star

A one-person company should feel like a small AI-native organization:

- CEO as strategy and judgment
- AI tools as execution teams
- CEO Console as command center, coordinator, scheduler, memory, and delivery manager
